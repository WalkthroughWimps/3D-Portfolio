About Me

I’m a creative technologist who blends design, animation, and code to craft delightful experiences. When preparing lessons as an English teacher, I was consistently disappointed by the resources I was able to find, and increasingly made my own to better suit my students' needs. This sparked a passion for creating captivating, interactive content that helped the students forget they were learning.

My creative output has run the gamut from educational games to animated explainers to engaging video content. I love solving problems with a blend of creativity and technical skill, and I'm always eager to learn new tools and techniques to expand my capabilities.

I excel at juggling multiple projects, meeting deadlines, and adapting to new challenges. As a teacher, I often needed to create multiple projects quickly and with little to no budget. With a very limited amount of planning time provided each week, I often worked from home to bring my creative visions to life.

I'm keen to put my skills to use in a dynamic environment where I can contribute to innovative projects and continue to grow as a creative technologist. I'm also curious how much more I can accomplish when I can devote myself full-time to creative work instead of balancing it with teaching.
